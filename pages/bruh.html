WHO ARE WE?

We're a bunch of weirdos that love dogs. We spend way too long tailoring our collars to fit each and every dog that takes our "Lucky Collar" quiz. We care about a few qualities here at Lucky:

STYLISH

We know a thing or two about dog fashion. If you buy a collar from us, your dog's gonna have swag.

HUMANITARIAN

There are a lot of puppies out there that don't have any homes :( We make sure to feed them every time a collar is purchased from our store (we donate two months of food to a local shelter!). We also have an app on both the Play Store and the App Store.

AFFORDABLE

Yo these collars are hella cheap bruh.
